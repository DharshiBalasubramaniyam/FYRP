Eligibility:

- Age below 12 years.
- Referral letter from a medical practicitioner or as referred by the medical officer at OPD.

Necessary Supporting documents:

- Child Health Development Record
- Past Medical Records
- Refferal Letters

Weekly Immunization schedule:

Age | Vaccine | Remarks

3 years | MMR (2 nd dose) | On completion of 3 years.
5 years | DT , OPV (5 th dose) | On completion of 5 years.
