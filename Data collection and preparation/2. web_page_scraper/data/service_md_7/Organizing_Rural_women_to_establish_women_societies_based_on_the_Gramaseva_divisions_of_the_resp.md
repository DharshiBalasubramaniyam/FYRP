

Objectives of establishing a women’s society

- Organizing Women’s Societies at grass-root level
- Improvement of leadership skills of women
- Mobilizing savings and credit facilities
- Training on financial recording
- Programs for exchange of experience among Women’s Societies
- Capacity building programs

Each Society which has been established should be registered under the Women’s Bureau, Minimum of 10 Women’s Societies has to be established within the respective division

Eligibility Any woman who is over 18 years old could join this Society Note: Minimum number of members for one Women’s Society should be 20

Cost: No cost involved

Fee:
