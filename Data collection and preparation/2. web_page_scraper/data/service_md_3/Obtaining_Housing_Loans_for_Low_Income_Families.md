Eligibility to Obtain a Housing Loan

- Low Income Family whose Monthly Income is Less than Rs 45,000/-
- Should be Below age 55
- Should Have Genuine Housing Requirement and Pre-preparation
- Should Have a Suitable Land for Housing

Procedure for Submitting [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s can be obtained on every working days at all NHDA District Offices and on Wednesdays from Housing Officers Attached to Divisional Secretariats.

[Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) Fee - Rs 10/- Perfected [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s can be Submitted During the Office Hours

Service Charge - 1%

Time Taken to Provide the Service One week (7 days)

Documents in Proof of Confirmation The Documents to prove the ownership of the Land: Income Certificate, Plan of the House and Approval of the Local Authority, Declaration of the Guarantors and other Requirements Mentioned in the [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) .
