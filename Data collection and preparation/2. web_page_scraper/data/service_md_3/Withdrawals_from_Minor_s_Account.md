Eligibility

Process of Submitting Applications Places From Where Application can be Obtained: Post Offices and Sub Post Offices

Fees Payable for Obtaining Application Forms : No fee is charged

Hours for Submitting Applications : From 09.00 a.m. to 15.00 p.m.

Fees Payable for Obtaining the Service : Nil

Time Taken to Providing the Service (Ordinary and Priority Service): 07 days

1. [Birth certificate](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=0&task=info&back=0&pid=727) of the minor child
2. [National Identity](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=367&task=info&back=0&pid=727) Card or letter certifying identity of the guardian
