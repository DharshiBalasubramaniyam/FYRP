Poverty Alleviation Micro Credit Project (Revolving Fund) Loan Scheme. Eligibility

- Should be organized as a group.
- Less than Rs.15,000/- monthly income
- Should not be a defaulter of a Bank or any other financial institution

Security

- Inter-se guarantee of two members of the group or any other suitable guarantee acceptable to the Bank.

Documents Required

1. Loan application
2. Guarantors' statements
3. Project report of the small project.

Kantha Ran Diriya For the facilities to Develop Small/Micro Scale Projects implemented by women. Eligibility

- Should be an account holder of the bank.
- Should not be a defaulter of the Bank or any other financial institutes.

Security

- Two guarantors acceptable to the Bank or any other security acceptable to the Bank.

Documents required

1. [Loan Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/)
2. Guarantors' statements or other documents, if property mortgage.

Comprehensive Credit Scheme for Small Enterprises Development (SED) To commence of expand small scale enterprises. Eligibility

1. Should be a account holder of the Bank
2. Be able to bring a minimum equity of 20% of the project cost.
3. Should not be a defaulter of the Bank or any other institution.

Security

- Inter-se guarantee of two borrowers under the scheme.
- Any other security

Documents Required

- [Loan Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/)
- Any other security
- Report of the Small Project

“Sookshama” (Micro) Credit Scheme Any income generating financially viable micro enterprises. Eligibility

1. Should maintain an account
2. Should not be a defaulter to the Bank or Any lending institution
3. Should be able to bring an equity of minimum 20% of the project cost

Security

- Inter-se guarantee of two borrowers or any two guarantors acceptable to the Bank.

Documents Required

1. [Loan Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/)
2. Guarantors' Statement
3. Small Project Report
