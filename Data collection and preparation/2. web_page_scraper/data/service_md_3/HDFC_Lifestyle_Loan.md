Purposes for HDFC Life Style Loan is granted

To purchase [home appliances](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=1900) from reputed suppliers to improve your lifestyle

Who can apply for the loan?

Group of employees of reputed mercantile establishments, corporations and government institutions can apply for this loan. A group should be comprise of at least 5 applicants.

The Loan amount that you can obtain

Up to 250,000/-

What about the guarantors?

Two guarantors employed in the same institution.



What is the repayment period?

Six months to three years.

What else? Savings Deposit of Rs. 500 /- should be opened with the Bank.
