Individual Savings/A Eligibility Criteria

- Sri Lankans over18 years of age

Required Documents

1. [National Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=416&task=info&back=0&pid=800) or Any other documentary
2. evidence for identification
3. Duly completed Mandate( [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) )
4. Signature Card
5. Initial Deposit

Time Duration for opening of the A/c

- 10 Minutes approx.

Limited Liabilities Company

Eligibility Criteria

- Daly Registered Certificate in Sri Lanka

Required Documents

1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. Memorandum for Articles
3. [Certificate of Registrar of Companies](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=482&task=info&back=0&pid=800)
4. Board Resolution
5. Form No 20 duly certified by Registrar of Companies
6. Duly completed Mandate( [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) )
7. Signature Card
8. Initial Deposit

Time Duration for opening of the A/c

- 20minutes approx.
