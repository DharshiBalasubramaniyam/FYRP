Kantha Ran Ginum Eligibility Criteria

- Sri Lankan Women over 18 years of age

Required Documents

1. [National Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=416&task=info&back=0&pid=802) or any other documents for identification
2. Duly completed Mandate ( [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) )
3. Initial deposit of Rs.500/-

Time Duration for opening of the A/c

- 10 Minutes approx.

Benefits

- Free Life & Medical Insurance Cover
- Credit Facility for self-employment
- Free Upsara Credit Card

‘Ethara Thilina Ginum’ S/A Eligibility Criteria

- Sri Lankan Migrant workers over 18 years of age

Required Documents

1. Photocopy of the [Pass Port](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=399&task=info&back=0&pid=802) / [National Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=416&task=info&back=0&pid=802)
2. Copy of the Certificate issued by Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment
3. Copy of the Visa
4. Duly completed mandate( [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) )

Time Duration for opening of the A/c

- 10 Minutes approx.

Benefits

- Initial deposit of Rs.200/-will be offered by the Bank
- Pre departure credit facilities for foreign employment

BOC Infinity Retirement Plan

* You can choose to receive BOC Infinity retirement benefits either from the age of 55 or 60 depending on your retirement date.
