1. Inform the Registrar of births and deaths at the General Hospital for registration of birth.
2. Appropriate persons for informing of a Birth for registration   
    - Father
    - Mother
    - Person present at the time of occurrence of the birth
    - Medical Officer of the Hospital

1. Obtain the relevant [declaration](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Particulars_for_registration_of_a_birth.pdf) from the Registrar of births and deaths at the General Hospital for informing the birth.
2. Documents to be submitted   
    - Duly perfected [declaration](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Particulars_for_registration_of_a_birth.pdf)
    - Hospital report issued in proof of the occurance of the Birth

1. A Birth can be registered free of charge within a period of 03 months.
2. A copy of the Birth Certificate is issued to the informant free of charge.
3. Visit the Additional District Registrar of the Divisional Secretariat at your nearest for further information.
