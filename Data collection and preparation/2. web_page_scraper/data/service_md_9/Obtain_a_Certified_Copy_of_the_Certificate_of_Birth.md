1. Refer the [application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Application_for_birth_certificate_or_search_registeres.pdf) to the Divisional Secretariat relevant to the area where the birth occurred.
2. Application can obtain from any Divisional Secretariat.
3. Fees chargeable   
    1. Where the date of registration or the no. of the entry is given the fee for one copy of the certificate is Rs. 100.00
    2. Where the date of registration or the no. of the entry is not given and a search of registers not exceeding two years is involved fee for one copy of the certificate is Rs. 200.00

1. Required fee should pay to the Divisional Secretariat.
2. Duly filled [application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Application_for_birth_certificate_or_search_registeres.pdf) should submit to the Divisional Secretariat.
3. If you like to obtain the certificate of birth by post, submit a stamped envelop with the [application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Application_for_birth_certificate_or_search_registeres.pdf) .
4. If the birth is registered a birth certificate will be send and if the birth is not registered a letter to the effect will be send.
