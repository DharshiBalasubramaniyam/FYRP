1. The Birth is registered by the Registrar of births and deaths of the area where birth occurred. If the birth has occurred at a private hospital, inform the Registrar of that area for registration of such birth.
2. Appropriate persons for informing of a birth for registration 
    1. Father
    2. Mother
    3. Person present at the time of occurrence of the birth
    4. Medical Officer of the Hospital

1. Obtain the relevant [declaration](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Particulars_for_registration_of_a_birth.pdf) from the Registrar of births and deaths for informing the birth.
2. Documents to be submitted 
    1. Duly perfected [declaration](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/Particulars_for_registration_of_a_birth.pdf)
    2. Hospital report issued in proof of the occurance of the birth

1. A birth can be registered free of charge within a period of 03 months.
2. A copy of the birth certificate is issued to the informant free of charge.
3. Visit the Additional District Registrar of the Divisional Secretariat at your nearest for further information.
