1. If a death has not been registered with 03 months since its occurrence it may be registered.
2. The death can only be registered only if a period of 25 yrs. has not been lapsed since occurrence of such death
3. Persons eligible for this, 
    1. Any party having interest of
    2. One of the parents
4. [Declaration](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/b15.pdf) can be produced by, 
    1. Close relation present at the time of death or who took care of the deceased when he/she was ill for the last time
    2. Any other party interested
5. Fee chargeable is Rs.50.00
