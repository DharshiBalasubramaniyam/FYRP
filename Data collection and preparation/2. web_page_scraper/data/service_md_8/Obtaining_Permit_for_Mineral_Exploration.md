Eligibility

- Be a Sri Lankan
- A foreigner who invests in Sri Lanka (having a registered company in Sri Lanka)
- Should not be a government officer.
- Should not be a mentally retarded, disable,or a person convicted by a court of law.

Process of Submitting Application

Places from where Application can be Obtained Head office

Fees Payable for Obtaining Application

Applications issued on free of charge.

Time at which Application should be Submitted During working hours.

Fees Payable for Obtaining the Service Depend on numbers of metric grid unit.

Time taken to Provide the Service (Ordinary Service and Priority Service) Depends on time taken to furnish documents. Supporting Documents Required

1. A project Proposal. 2. Professional / Technical credentials of project 3. Metrict designation of area requested team. 4. Proof of financial capability to carry out explorations.
