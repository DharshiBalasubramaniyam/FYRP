Obtaining Assistance for the Improvement of productivity in Low Productive EAC Lands

Who can apply: Farmers or Estates growing Cinnamon, Pepper, Coffee, Cocoa, Cardamom, Clove, Nutmeg and have low productive lands with over 25% crop vacancies in their lands

Assistance Provided by the DEA under the Scheme:

- Required no. of planting material, based on vacancies in land, at free of charge
- Technical advices
- Financial reward or fertilizer after the successful completion of recommended practices
- Eligibility Criteria:
- Minumum eligible land extent - 0.2 ha (1/2Ac.)
- Minumum eligible land extent for " Jathika Saviya" or "Gama Naguma" programs - 0.1 ha (1/2Ac.)
- [Title of the land](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=1161&task=info&back=0&pid=827) must be produced as a prooff of?the ownership of the land. [Plan of the land](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=827) is necessary for lands over 2 ha
