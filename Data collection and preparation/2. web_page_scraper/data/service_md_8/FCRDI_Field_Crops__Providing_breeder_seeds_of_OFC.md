Who can obtain the breeder seeds Breeder seeds are produced and handover to Seed and Planting Material Division. Seeds of parental lines of maize hybrid variety “Samapth” can be obtained by private companies and any government seed farms who are interested in producing hybrid seeds





The procedure of purchasing maize parental lines Request should be made in writing to Director, Field crop Research and Development Institute, Mahailuppallama. Signing of an agreement to produce hybrid seeds with the Director General of Agriculture/ Department of Agriculture. Technical knowhow will be given from the FCRDI to the seed producers







From where maize parental lines can be purchased Contact Director, Field Crops Research and Development Institute, Mahailuppallama
