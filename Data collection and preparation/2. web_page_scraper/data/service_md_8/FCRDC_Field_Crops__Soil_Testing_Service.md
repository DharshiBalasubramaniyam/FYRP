Submitting the soil sample for testing

�

The AI handover the soil sample to the laboratory through the Director/ Deputy Director of the institute. Soil sample preparation for testing at the laboratory and testing for the following soil parameter only : pH, Electrical conductivity (EC), Available Phosphorus content, Exchangeable Potassium content, Organic matter content, texture and the micro nutrient content

Reports produced: soil test report, soil test based fertilizer recommendation report with necessary instructions on soil fertility management

�



Time taken for issuing the report

�

3- 4 weeks after receiving the soil sample to the laboratory
