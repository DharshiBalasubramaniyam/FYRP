Submission Procedure Making payments Counter – Cashier Submission of Indent Form, Request Letter and Documents with supported documents. Counter – Unit 01

Procedure

Step 1: Applicant should submit request letter to Unit 01 for Block License. Step 2: Unit 01 examines importation history of the applicant for the past 03 years. Step 3: Unit 01 directs the request to the Assistant Controller. Step 4: Assistant Controller approves the request. Step 5: Unit 01 charges the licensing fee of 0.1 % of the Invoice Value. Step 6: Unit 01 issues the Block License.

Note: If Assistant Controller is not satisfied with importation history of the applicant, request will be rejected.
