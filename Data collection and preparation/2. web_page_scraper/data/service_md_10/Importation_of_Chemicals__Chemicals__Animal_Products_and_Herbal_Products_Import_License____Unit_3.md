Obtaining License

Eligibility Applicant should have the [Business Registration Certificate](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=482&task=info&back=0&pid=428) Applicant should have the approval from the relevant authority

Support Documents Required

- Four copies of the Proforma Invoices
- [Business Registration Certificate](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=482&task=info&back=0&pid=428)
- 4 copies of the indent form
- Approval letter from relevant authority

Submission Procedure Obtain Application Forms Counter – Unit 03

Making Payments Counter – Cashier

Submission of Application Form Request Letter and Support Documents Counter – Unit 03

Application Form : Specimen [Indent Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/)

Procedure

Step 1: Obtain the indent form from Unit 3 Step 2: Fill the indent form and submit it to Unit 3 with the relevant support documents. Step 3: Unit 03 checks the request detail and checks the validity of information. Step 4: When unit 03 is satisfied with the request, the license fee is charged from the applicant. Step 5: Applicant pays the license fee to the Cashier Step 6: Forward the request detail to the Import/Export Controller for final approval Step 7: Import/Export Controller approves the request Step 8: Unit 03 computerizes the license detail Step 9: Unit 03 issues the License after the approval.

Note: If unit 03 is not satisfied with the information provided, the applicant has to provide the lacking information.If Unit 03 is satisfied with the information provided, it is directed to the Import/Export Controller.

Time Line

Process Time Line : One Day

Validity Time Line : The validity period for this service varies from 1 to 6 months.

Costs Related to the Service

Cost : No fee is charged

Fee: 0.2 % of the CIF value

Penalties : If the importer has imported goods without any license or if he has violatedby the licensing conditions a fine is imposed on him.

Note: If the importer has imported goods without a license, the Import/Export Controller charges an additional fee as a penalty.

Surcharge: No Surcharge.

- Customer Documents/Invoice
