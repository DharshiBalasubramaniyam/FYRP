Eligibility Any interested individual Manufacturer /Trader /group of Manufacturers /Traders can make a request to the Authority.

Submission Procedure Request should be made to the Director General of the Consumer Affairs Authority.

Procedure Step 1: Manufacturer /Trader places a request to the Director General Consumer Affairs Authority Step 2: Discuss with the applicant to identify the type of the awareness program needed by the applicant Step 3: Design the program Step 4: Conducts the Awareness Program Step 5: Discussion with Manufacturer /Trader and obtain their feed back Step 6: Monitoring

Time Line Process Time Line: Applicants have to send the request 1 month ahead of the scheduled date of the proposed program

Validity Time Line: There is no specific validity time live.

Cost Related to the Services Cost : Has to be born by the applicant Fee: A fee will be charged based on the costs incurred by the Authority.

Special Cases Apart from conducting market investigations and raids Consumer Affairs Authority on its own motion conduct awareness programs for Manufactures /Traders.

- Authority selects a particular area to conduct the awareness programs
- Officers visit the traders/manufactures within that area and conduct awareness programs
- After the awareness program, Investigation team re-visits the particular traders to investigate whether they follow the rules and regulations of the CAA.
- If the traders violate those rules and regulations, legal action will be taken.
