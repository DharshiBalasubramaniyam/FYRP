[Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) : Specimen Indent Form

Procedure Step 1: Importer obtains [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) from Unit 4/5. Step 2: The applicant fills the [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) . Step 3: The Request letter is obtained from the relevant authority. Step 4: [Application Form](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) is submitted to Unit 4/5 with support documents. Step 5: The Head of the Division examines the documents handed over. Step 6: Applicant pays license fees to the Cashier. Step 7: License is issued by Unit 4/5.
