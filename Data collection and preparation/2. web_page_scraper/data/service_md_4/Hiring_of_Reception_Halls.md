Pradeepa Halls at Gunasinghepura, Modara and Maligawatta are hired for rent to public for Weddings, other Occasions and exhibitions and Conferences . ( Halls are not Hired for Funerals).

Procedure of Obtaining [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s

From where to Obtain [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s can be obtained from the NHDA Colombo City North Office at Maligawatta.   On paying money after Handing over the completed [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) ,Relevant Hall can be Allocated for the specific day and time if not already Allocated.

Fees Payable to Obtain [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s: [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) s are provided free of charge.

When to Submit [Application](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/pdf/) : From 8.30 a.m. to 3.00 p.m. Fees Payable to Obtain Service : Relevant format attached.

Duration to Provide Service (Normal and Prime services) Within 15 minutes.

Documents Required in Proof The person who wants to allocate the Hall should be present and he has to submit his [Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=0&task=info&back=0&pid=674) .
