- The acceprate financial should the one maintained is circular no: 2007/3 issued by the Land Commissioner General (the mortgaging Institution)
- Should be a blood relation
- Should have obtain the approval of the relevant Divisional Secretary

Method of Submitting Application:

Mortgaging: the application prepared by you should be submitted Transferring: same has above
