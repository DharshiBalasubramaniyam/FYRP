Eligibility: Schools, Government and Non-government institutes.

Application Procedure:

Application Counters: Reserve a date by sending a letter to the Director General or Instrument Branch or arriving to the department.

Payments for Application Forms: None

Time to Submit the Application Forms: 9:00am to 3:00pm on weekdays

Service Charges: It is decided on exhibits, officers and the institute that the service is needed.

The Time for the Service (Normal and Prime Services): Not necessary

Required Documents: A letter from the head of the school or institute that the service is needed.
