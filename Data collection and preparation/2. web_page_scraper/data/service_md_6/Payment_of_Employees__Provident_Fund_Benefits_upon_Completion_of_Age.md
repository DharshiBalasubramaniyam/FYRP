Eligibility

If a male employee, 55 years of age should be completed.

If a female employee, 50 years of age should be completed.

N:B: It is compulsory to have been left the employment at the time of claiming for benefits.



Counter on the ground floor of the Head office, all District offices and Sub Labour offices and website [labourdept.gov.lk](https://labourdept.gov.lk/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=67&Itemid=61&lang=en)

No charge

Completed application form should, by the member himself/herself be handed over to the nearest District labour office or Labour sub office where he/she resides, during the office hours of a working day.

No service charge

03 weeks, if all the document are completed

Application certified by the last employer, “B” cards relevant to membership numbers, [National Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=416&task=info&back=0&pid=846) and a copy of the [National Identity Card](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=416&task=info&back=0&pid=846) certified by the employer, copy of the bank pass book Original birth certificate and National Identity card should be produced. Valid driving license/ valid passport/ Adult identity card can be produced in the absence of National Identity card If the birth is not registered or the relevant documents are damaged, the results of search of registers should be produced along with an affidavit to confirm the probable age
