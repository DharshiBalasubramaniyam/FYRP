Procedure for Submitting of Applications There is no specific application form for registration with the “Central [Job Bank](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=1273) ”, and this is an entirely an online process.

The service of the “Central [Job Bank](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=1273) ” could be obtained through the [Job Bank](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=1273) Website, [www.jobbank.lk](https://www.jobbank.lk) .

The Job-seeker him/herself could register with the [Job Bank](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=&task=info&back=0&pid=1273) , and he/she could obtain necessary information on the availability of vacancies as well. Furthermore, the Job-seeker him/herself could forward his/her application or contact directly to the place where vacancies exist.
