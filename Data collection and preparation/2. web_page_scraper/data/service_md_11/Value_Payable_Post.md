Eligibility In regard to money orders issued for Value Payable Post parcels, sender of the parcel or any person connected with it.

Process of Submitting Applications Places From Where Application Forms can be Obtained From Post Offices and Sub Post Offices.

Fees Payable for Obtaining Application Forms: Nil

Hours for Submission: From post offices from 9:00 a.m to 3:00 p.m on week days from 9:00 to 1:00 p.m on Saturdays

Fees Payable for obtaining the Service : Stamp fees for the parcel

Time Taken to Provide the Service (Ordinary Service and Priority Services) Within 03 days from the date of delivery of the parcel to the recipient

Supporting Document Required : V.P.P Money Order
