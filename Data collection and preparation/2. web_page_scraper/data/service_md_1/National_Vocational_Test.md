Eligibility Grade III - Successfully completed accepted training , one year or not less than six month in any vocational. Grade II - Two years experience after passing grade III, or successfully completed 2 years training. Grade I - Cannot apply directly for grade one. Those who have 3 years experience after passing grade II level can apply for grade one.
