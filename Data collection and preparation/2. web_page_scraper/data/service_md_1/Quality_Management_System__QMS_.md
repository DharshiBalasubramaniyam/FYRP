As a prime requirement, TVEC is to assure the quality and relevance of TVET sector. In order to achieve this objective TVEC [register institutes](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=938&task=info&back=0&pid=1267) and [accredit training](https://gic.gov.lk/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?id=941&task=info&back=0&pid=1267) programmes. Training institutions which are registered under TVEC and conducting accredited training courses are expected to be implemented and maintained specified Quality Management System (QMS), that is ISO 9001:2000. TVEC is making arrangements to award ISO 9001:2000 in order to assist the institutes for continual improvement of processes to achieve customer (Student/Trainee, Employer) satisfaction.
